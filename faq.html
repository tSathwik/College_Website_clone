What is the history of BVRIT?

BVRIT Narsapur is an engineering college located in Narsapur, a town in the Indian state of Telangana. The college was established in 1997 and is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH).
BVRIT Narsapur was founded by Sri Vishnu Educational Society (SVES), a non-profit organization established in 1991 with the aim of promoting education in the fields of engineering, technology, and management. The society has been instrumental in establishing several educational institutions in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.
BVRIT Narsapur started with three undergraduate engineering courses in 1997: Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), and Information Technology (IT). Over the years, the college has expanded its course offerings to include postgraduate programs in engineering and management, as well as research programs.
Today, BVRIT Narsapur is recognized as one of the top engineering colleges in Telangana and has been accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with an "A" grade. The college has also been ranked among the top 150 engineering colleges in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF).
